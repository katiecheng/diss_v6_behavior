Stanford AAA Lab

In the following activity, you will proceed through 3 stages:

  1. First, you will see 24 Swahili words paired with their English translations.
  2. Then, you will be asked to study the 24 Swahili-English word pairs. The word pairs will be split into three sets of eight. You will be asked to use a different study strategy for each set.
  3. Finally, you will be quizzed on all 24 Swahili-English word pairs. On the quiz, you will be given the Swahili word, and asked to type the English translation.
You will be reminded of these instructions along the way.

Stanford AAA Lab

Welcome! In the following activity, you will be asked to learn 40 Swahili words and their English translations. You will learn these words in two rounds, 20 words at a time. Each round has 3 phases:

  1. Presentation phase: You will be introduced to 20 Swahili words.
  2. Learning phase: You will study each Swahili word.
  3. Quiz phase: You will be quizzed on what you've learned. On the quiz, you will be shown the Swahili word, and asked to type the English translation.
In total, across the two halves, you will learn 40 Swahili words and their English translations.

You will be reminded of these instructions along the way. Let's get started!
